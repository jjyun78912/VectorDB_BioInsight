üí° Interpretation

The large number of DEGs may reflect broad transcriptional changes associated with the comparison groups. This is consistent with heterogeneous biological states but makes identification of specific functional drivers challenging without additional evidence. Hub genes were identified that are not in cancer databases. These may represent novel candidates, but database absence could also reflect limited prior study rather than biological novelty. Functional validation would be required to assess their significance.

  • RNA-seq measures transcript abundance, not protein levels or activity.

  • Pathway enrichment shows statistical association, not mechanistic causation.
